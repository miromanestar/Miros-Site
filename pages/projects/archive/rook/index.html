Rook

A simple online multiplayer card game made using google firebase.

What is Rook?

The game consists of 3-6 players, and each player must keep their hand secret. The object of the game is to be the first player to reach 500 points by capturing cards with a point value in tricks. If more than 1 player attain more than 500 points in the same trick, the winning player is the one with the highest trick. Only certain cards have a point value. These are known as counters. 5s are worth 5 points, 10s and 14s are worth 10 points, 1s are worth 15 points, and the rook card is worth 20 points. Finally, the player who wins the last trick of a round gets an extra 20 points. For a comprehensive explanation of the rules, please click here.
