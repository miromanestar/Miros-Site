What is SAU Missions Dashboard?

The SAU Missions Dashboard is a simple website built for a large touch-screen contained in a large room. It allows users to easily see information relating to student missions at Southern Adventist University, including current student missionaries, SAU's presence around the globe, and opportunities currently available for students here.
